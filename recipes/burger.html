BURGER

Description

This recipe features a lean poultry burger that stays incredibly juicy and flavorful. Ground meat is mixed with seasoned breadcrumbs, finely diced onion, fresh parsley, garlic, egg whites, and simple seasonings, then shaped into patties and pan-seared until perfectly cooked.

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds ground turkey
  • ¼ cup seasoned bread crumbs
  • ¼ cup finely diced onion
  • 2 egg whites, lightly beaten
  • ¼ cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 clove garlic, peeled and minced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Mix ground turkey, seasoned bread crumbs, onion, egg whites, parsley, garlic, salt, and pepper together in a large bowl.
  3. Form into 12 patties.
  4. Cook the patties in a medium skillet over medium heat, turning once, to an internal temperature of 180 degrees F (85 degrees C).
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!
