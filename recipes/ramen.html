Description

A quick and indulgent comfort food snack that transforms instant noodles into a creamy burger alternative. Cooked noodles are tossed directly into a fast, savory sauce made from milk, butter, soy sauce, and melted American cheese, often finished with a fried egg and sliced green onions.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups water
  • 1 (3 ounce) package any flavor ramen noodles
  • 1 slice American cheese

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Bring water to a boil in a saucepan. Add ramen noodles and cook until tender, about 2 minutes.
  3. Pour out water, then stir in seasoning packet and cheese until well blended.
